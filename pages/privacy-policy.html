Privacy Policy.

Your data belongs to you. At Banana, we specialise in keeping your information safe while helping you find the best deals. Privacy is our core ingredient.

1. Data Collection

When you sign up to Banana, we collect basic information like your name and email address. We also collect data regarding your preferences to help our AI specialise your deal recommendations.

2. How We Use It

Your information is used purely for organising your personalised dashboard and improving our decision engine. We do not sell your data to third-party marketers. We only share details with providers when you explicitly choose to "split" and switch a service.

3. Security Standards

We utilise modern encryption and secure PostgreSQL databases to protect your identity. All data transfers are authorised through secure protocols to ensure no one slips through our security layers.

4. Your Rights

Under UK data protection laws, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data at any time. Simply contact us if you wish to exercise these rights or want more information on how we manage your privacy.
