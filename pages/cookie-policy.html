1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help Banana remember your preferences and keep you signed in to your dashboard.

2. How We Use Them

We use cookies to optimise site performance and understand how you interact with our decision engine. These are categorised into essential cookies (required for the site to work) and analytical cookies (which help us improve the bunch).

3. Your Control

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit your ability to use certain features on Banana. By continuing to use our platform without changing your settings, you consent to our use of cookies as described here.
